Prague

Places and sights I have been to and recommend.

Hotel Opera

One of the first things I saw as I left my hotel was Hotel Opera. I loved the bold pink colour, which inspired the pink used throughout my website.

Deep Fried Camembert

This is a meal I ate in Prague — deep fried camembert with tartar sauce and salad. It was delicious and I can see why it’s considered a comfort food.

Pin Map

This pin map was in the downstairs area of the food place. Visitors pin where they are from, and it was overwhelmingly Europe. I loved the texture and interactive element.

River Cruise

My family and I went on a river cruise at dusk. It was beautiful seeing the city light up from the water.

Meet Burger

After the cruise, we wandered the city and found Meet Burger. I had wings and a burger, and they even served beer in a three-pint glass!

Goosey

On the last day, I bought one of these goose toys I kept seeing in shops. He was huge and difficult to fit in my suitcase — but totally worth it.

Prague Travel Video

Here is a video I found of a person walking through Prague. Before I went on my trip, I found it helpful to see the city briefly beforehand as it helps with navigation and understanding the vibe of the city.
